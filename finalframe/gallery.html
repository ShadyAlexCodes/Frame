GALLERY

Content Heavy

This template balances useful image space with text space in order to effectively communicate as much information to your audience as possible. Each section will separate your information into manageable chunks of information for your audience to follow.

Text Heavy

The text heavy template is best used for informational sites with the purpose of educating audiences. However, we know text can be quite boring, so this template effectively balances the text with images.

Graphic Heavy

This template is perfect for users looking to showcase many graphic works on their site. We recommend this template for blog galleries, portfolio demos, and other image-heavy websites. Like always, you have full control over what images/content appears in these sections.

Gallery

The gallery is full of 9x9 gallery sections that you can fill with your own custom images. This is a great template to showcase vacation pictures, family photos, and other things of that nature. Under all of the images there's a section for descriptions.

Organized Variety

This section perfectly balances images and text to deliver the best possible content to your audience. This section is also logically balanced, presenting a general overview that naturally flows into more detailed images/information.

Text and Image Blend

This template allows for the greatest amount of creative freedom after download. You'll be able to transform simple text sections into more complex layouts and craft unique creations using the image sections.
